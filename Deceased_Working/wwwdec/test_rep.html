Histogram of Days to report(of deceased patients) across Districts




The histograms of days to report of the deceased patients has been presented here across districts, as per the Media Bulletin published by the Karnataka state government, we calculated the difference of decease date and media bulletin date for each patient and we call the difference as days to report and then we plot the frequency histogram wave wise allowing us a comparative study between individual districts about how rapidly deaths are being reported.

For all the graphs on this page, if you click on the image then it will display an interactive graph, where as you hover your mouse pointer over the graph annotations with details will be displayed.

Analysing Wave-Wise

The entire data collected has been analysed wave wise to get a better understanding of the impact of Covid-19 and the nature of victims.

  • Wave 1 - till the end of October 2020
  • Wave Middle -From Nov-2020 to Jan-2021
  • Wave 2 - since the end of January 2021 till present

Since,the counts during the Wave Middle were comparatively low,so,we only considered Wave 1 and Wave 2 for our analysis..
