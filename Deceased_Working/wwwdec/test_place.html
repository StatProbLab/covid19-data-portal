Barplots of Place of Death(of deceased patients) across Districts




The bar plots of Place of Death of the deceased patients has been presented here across districts.As per the Media Bulletin published by the Karnataka state government, the individual deaths were reported as Hospitalised or Brought Dead or Died at residence,we then took the counts and plot a barplot.

For all the graphs on this page, if you click on the image then it will display an interactive graph, where as you hover your mouse pointer over the graph annotations with details will be displayed.
