Karnataka Deceased Data-Wave-wise Analysis

Karnataka State Government's Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has a dedicated COVID-19 Dashboard on its website and also has been providing detailed Media Bulletins on a daily basis since March 9th, 2020. In this page, we track the Deceased data provided in bulletins to give a more comprehensive view of the COVID-19 fatalities in the state of Karnataka. In particular we track:

  • Deaths across Waves
  • Age-Distribution
  • Age-Gender Distribution
  • Days to Decease
  • Days to Report
  • Place of Death

The data collected has been classified into three waves to get a better understanding of the impact of Covid-19.

  • Wave 1 - till the end of October 2020
  • Middle wave - from November 2020 till the end of January 2021
  • Wave 2 - from February 2021 till present

Since,the counts during the Wave Middle were comparatively low,so,for some of the plots,we only considered Wave 1 and Wave 2 for our analysis.
