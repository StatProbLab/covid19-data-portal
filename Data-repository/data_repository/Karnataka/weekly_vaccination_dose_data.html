Name of the CSV File

weekly_vaccination_dose_data.csv

Basic Description

This csv file contains information about weekly dose 1 and dose 2 vaccination across all districts in Karnataka.

Data Duration

28-02-22 to 28-02-22

List of Column Names with Meanings

Column Name Meaning
District List of all 31 districts of the Karnataka state.
Dose1-% Vaccine dose 1 percentage with respect to adult population.
Dose2-% Vaccine dose 2 percentage with respect to adult population.
PrecDose-% Precaution Vaccine dose percentage with respect to adult population.
Dose1-count Number of people who have taken 1st dose of vaccine.
Dose2-count Number of people who have taken 2nd dose of vaccine.
PrecDose-count Number of people who have taken precaution dose of vaccine.
Population-2020 Projected population for the year of 2020.
Population-2011-census Population(18+) according to the census of the year 2011.
Factor-used Factor used to obtain the projected population from the census population.
Cowin-Population Population which have taken Cowin vaccine.
Cow-Dose1 Cowin Vaccine dose 1 percentage with respect to adult population.
Cow-Dose2 Cowin Vaccine dose 2 percentage with respect to adult population.
Cow-PrecDose Cowin Precaution Vaccine dose percentage with respect to adult population.
Date For the date of 28-02-22 this data is taken.
